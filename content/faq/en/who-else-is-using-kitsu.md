## Built for the Whole Studio, Trusted at Every Level

Kitsu is embraced by the people doing the work:

- Producers use it to track budgets, forecast costs, and generate client-ready reports
- Directors & supervisors rely on it for playlist reviews, annotation tools, and version comparison
- Artists keep their todo lists current and submit previews without leaving their DCC tool
- Production coordinators manage task dispatching, scheduling, and resource allocation all in one view

Things stop falling through the cracks when the whole team is in the same system.
