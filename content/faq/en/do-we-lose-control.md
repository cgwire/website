# You Don't Lose Control. You Finally Have It.

If your studio runs on spreadsheets, shared drives, or internal tools stitched together over the years, switching to a new platform can feel like handing the keys to someone else. That concern is valid — and it's exactly why Kitsu was built the way it is.

## The Hidden Cost of "Familiar" Tools

Spreadsheets feel controllable because you built them. But ask yourself: who actually knows how they work? When that one producer leaves, does the logic leave with them?

This is tribal knowledge risk — and it's one of the most common ways studios lose control quietly, over time. Critical information lives in someone's head, a local file, or an inbox thread nobody can find.

Kitsu turns implicit knowledge into structured, visible, searchable data — owned by your studio, not by any individual.

## Permissions That Mirror Your Studio

Kitsu's role-based permissions let you define exactly who sees what and who can do what — at the project level, the department level, and the task level.

- **Producers and managers** get full visibility across all projects and teams.
- **Supervisors** see and act on their departments without noise from others.
- **Artists** see only their assignments — their to-do list, their feedback, their deadlines.

No more "oops, someone edited the wrong thing." No more wondering who changed a status and when.

## Every Action Is Logged

Kitsu maintains a complete audit trail across your production. Every status change, every comment, every preview upload, every revision — timestamped and attributed to the person who made it.

If something goes wrong (or right), you can trace it. That's more control than any spreadsheet ever gave you.

## Visibility Across Every Project, All at Once

With spreadsheets, getting a cross-project view means manually consolidating files. With Kitsu, it's built in.

Production reports and dashboards give you a 360° view across all active productions — asset progress, shot readiness, team workload, budget forecasts — without chasing anyone for an update.

Your managers stop being information brokers. They start making decisions.

## Your Data, Your Way

Kitsu is open-source. Your production data is exposed through a full REST API and Python client, so you can export, integrate, or migrate it at any time. There is no lock-in.

Whether you choose cloud hosting, on-premise deployment on your own servers, or a custom architecture tailored to your infrastructure — you stay in control of where your data lives.

**Control isn't about the tool you're used to. It's about knowing what's happening, who did what, and what comes next.** Kitsu gives your whole studio that clarity — without depending on the one person who built the spreadsheet.
