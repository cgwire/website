# "Can Kitsu Scale With Us?"

**Short answer: Yes — and it's built specifically for that.**

Whether you're a 5-person studio wrapping up your first short film or a 200-person production house juggling three simultaneous series, Kitsu grows with you at every stage. You won't outgrow it. You won't need to switch.

---

## Built for Where You Are *and* Where You're Going

Most studios start small. They cobble together spreadsheets, shared drives, and chat threads — and it works, until it doesn't.

Kitsu is designed to be adopted early and stay useful forever. Small teams get a clean, fast setup that doesn't require a dedicated pipeline TD to maintain. Large productions get the depth they need: multi-project dashboards, granular permissions, custom workflows, and enterprise-grade infrastructure.

The platform scales in every direction:

- **Team size** — from a handful of artists to hundreds across multiple departments
- **Project volume** — manage one short or ten simultaneous productions from a single workspace
- **Pipeline complexity** — simple task tracking on day one, full API and DCC integrations when you're ready

---

## Multi-Project Management, Without the Chaos

As studios grow, the real challenge isn't managing one production — it's managing several at once without losing visibility.

Kitsu gives producers and supervisors a cross-project view so nothing falls through the cracks. Track progress, spot bottlenecks, and reallocate resources across productions without switching between tools or exporting data into a spreadsheet.

When you land a new project, you spin it up inside the same system your team already knows. No new tool to learn. No migration. No lag.

## Infrastructure That Grows With Your Ambitions

Scaling isn't just about features — it's about reliability and control.

Kitsu offers three deployment paths depending on where your studio is:

| Stage | Option | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Early / lean | Cloud (SaaS) | Up and running in minutes, no ops overhead |
| Growing | On-Premise | Full deployment on your own servers, managed by CGWire |
| Enterprise | Tailor-Made | Multi-instance setups, Active Directory, GPU video clusters, local object storage |

You move between these as your needs evolve. CGWire's ops team handles the transitions.
